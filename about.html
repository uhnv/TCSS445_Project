About Us

Here at Husky Market, we strive to provide an easy and efficient platform for students to buy and sell school supplies. Our goal is to create a community where students can connect and help each other out, while also getting great deals on the items they need.

Our Team

Meet the team behind Husky Market! We are a group of Computer Science seniors at University of Washington - Tacoma. Three of us are Vietnamese and one is from Belarus. We share a passion for creating innovative solutions that make a positive impact on people's lives.

Our Goals

  • Provide a Convenient Platform: Our goal is to provide an easy and efficient platform for students to buy school supplies.
  • Create a Community: We strive to create a community where students can connect and help each other out, fostering a positive and supportive environment.
  • Improve Student Life: Our platform aims to make student life easier by providing a one-stop-shop for buying school supplies.
  • Provide a Safe Environment: We want to ensure the safety of our users by implementing secure login and payment systems.
